What is Cornerstone Payment Systems?

Cornerstone Payment Systems is a Christian Credit Card Processing company founded in 2001 with the goal to honor the Lord in all its processing.

How do you save me money?

We provide a free rate analysis of your current credit card processing to show you the savings based off the various card types your business accepts.

What are your rates?

We don’t have set rates because our goal is to tailor a solution that will save your organization money no matter your rates.

How much can you save me?

Savings vary based on the volume and card type used. Our largest savings to date is over $7,000 a month!

What is credit card processing?

Credit card processing is what allows a business or non-profit to accept credit cards by transmitting information and funds between consumers.
